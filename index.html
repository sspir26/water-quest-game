Water Quest

Help bring clean water to communities in need by tapping the yellow jerry cans as fast as you can. Avoid the dirty cans – they represent unsafe water.

Score 0
Time Left 30s

Press Start Game to begin. Tap clean yellow cans to earn points!

Clean water (yellow jerry can) = +10 points
Dirty water (brown can) = −5 points

Inspired by charity: water and their mission to bring clean and safe drinking water to people around the world.
