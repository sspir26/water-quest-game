charity: water (fan project)

Water Quest

Catch as many clean water drops as you can before time runs out. Each drop represents people getting closer to safe, clean water.

How to Play

  1. Choose a difficulty level.
  2. Click Start Game.
  3. Click the blue water drops that appear in the game area.
  4. Try to reach or beat the goal before time runs out!

Difficulty

Difficulty: easy
Goal: 5
Score: 0
Time left: 20s

Press Start Game.

Why clean water?

charity: water works to bring safe, clean drinking water to communities around the world. Water affects health, education, and opportunity.

This small game is a playful reminder that every “drop” matters. In real life, reliable clean water can change the course of someone’s day, year, and future.
