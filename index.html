Water Quest

Collect clean water drops before time runs out and help bring safe water to communities in need.

How to Play

  1. Choose a difficulty: Easy, Normal, or Hard.
  2. Click the Start Game button.
  3. Tap or click as many blue water drops as you can before time runs out.
  4. Hit the goal to “fund” a virtual water project!

Difficulty

Difficulty: Easy
Goal: 8 drops
Score: 0
Time left: 30s

Choose a difficulty and press “Start Game” to begin.

Why Water?

1 in 10 people worldwide live without access to clean water. This game is a small reminder of how important clean water is — and how every action can help.
